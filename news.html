News & Updates

Updates from across the RMD UK network — chapters, governance, and the wider near-peer BLS community.

30 Jun 2026

RMD UK website relaunched

This site has been rebuilt to better reflect RMD UK as a national network of chapters, with a dedicated home for institutions interested in joining and a single, network-wide faculty directory. Several chapter pages remain templates pending content from each site — see individual chapter pages for status.

2026

Near-peer model adopted into the RCUK BLS portfolio

The near-peer Basic Life Support model developed by RMD UK chapters has been adopted into the Resuscitation Council UK's BLS portfolio. RMD UK remains the independent community of chapters that delivers and develops this model — not a branch of RCUK — and is working with RCUK on a framework for new chapters joining the network. Read more about this relationship.

In progress

RMD Plymouth chapter in development

A new chapter is being established at the University of Plymouth, joining Birmingham, Bristol, Warwick, and Brunel in the RMD UK network. Visit the RMD Plymouth page for the latest status, or get in touch if you're involved in setting it up.
