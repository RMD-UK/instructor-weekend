About

RMD at the University of Bristol

RMD Bristol delivers near-peer Basic Life Support training to healthcare students at the University of Bristol, following the model developed at RMD Birmingham in 1995. RMD Bristol is part of the RMD UK network, working with other chapters on shared standards and faculty development.

The Programme

The near-peer pathway at Bristol

RMD Bristol follows the same development pathway used across the RMD UK network.

1

Candidate

Receives BLS training from near-peer instructors.

2

Instructor

Trains to teach BLS to the next cohort of candidates.

3

Assessor

Quality-assures teaching and signs off competency.

4

Senior Faculty

Leads chapter governance and faculty development.
