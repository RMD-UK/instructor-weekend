Research

Evidence and evaluation from the RMD UK network — near-peer BLS education, instructor development, and outcomes.

Research lead

Joe Alderman, RMD Birmingham

Research within the RMD UK network is coordinated through RMD Birmingham, with Joe Alderman as research lead. Work focuses on the effectiveness of the near-peer teaching model, BLS skill retention, and the development pathway from candidate to assessor. Cross-site research is being developed as the network grows.
