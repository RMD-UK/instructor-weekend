What this form is for

This form is for institutions who have already had an initial conversation with RMD UK — usually via the enquiry form — and are ready to take the next step. Submitting this form does not create a binding agreement; it tells us you'd like to proceed and gives us the detail we need to draft an MOU for your institution to review.

Haven't spoken to us yet? Start with the general enquiry form instead — it's a better fit for early-stage questions.

Submit MOU details

A member of RMD UK's governance group will follow up to confirm details and share a draft MOU.

We aim to respond within 5 working days. Your details will only be used to progress this MOU enquiry.
