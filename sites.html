Our Network

RMD UK is a collegiate national community, not a centralised body. Four chapters are live, with a fifth in development.

Founding Chapter · Est. 1995

RMD Birmingham

University of Birmingham, College of Medical and Dental Sciences

Live

RMD: Resuscitation | Mentorship | Development was established in 1995 by three medical students at the University of Birmingham to provide Basic Life Support training to healthcare students through a near-peer, interdisciplinary programme.

Now overseen by a senior faculty of clinical and non-clinical staff — including one of the founders, Prof Jon Hulme — and run by a student faculty of healthcare undergraduates, this near-peer course is one of the largest of its kind in Europe. Cited by the General Medical Council (GMC) as an innovative teaching method and endorsed by the Faculty of Intensive Care Medicine.

Each year, RMD Birmingham teaches over 1,000 candidates from eight different courses at UoB, with a team of 65 BLS instructors and 20 assessors. The community arm, MiLE, delivers BLS training across Birmingham and beyond.

RMD Bristol

University of Bristol

Live

RMD Bristol delivers near-peer Basic Life Support training at the University of Bristol, building on the model developed at RMD Birmingham to train healthcare undergraduates.

As part of the RMD UK network, RMD Bristol benefits from shared standards, cross-chapter collaboration, and the collective expertise of the wider community.

RMD UK Chapter

RMD Warwick

University of Warwick

Live

RMD Warwick is part of the RMD UK network at the University of Warwick, delivering near-peer resuscitation education across healthcare specialties.

The chapter follows the same near-peer pathway used across the network — training instructors, assessors, and future leaders within a supportive national community.

RMD Brunel

Brunel University London

Live

RMD Brunel brings the near-peer approach to Brunel University London, fostering a student-led culture of resuscitation training and professional development.

Established with the support of the wider RMD UK network, the Brunel chapter has benefited directly from the mentorship that distinguishes the RMD community.
