Candidate Pre-Course Information
RMD Instructor Weekend 2026

We look forward to welcoming you to the RMD Instructor Weekend. This brief introductory letter contains essential details and some pre-course requirements relevant to you as a candidate for the course.

Course dates: 0800h Saturday 10 October — 1700h Sunday 11 October 2026
Registration: Please arrive by 0800h on Saturday 10 October.
Venue: Birmingham Medical School, University of Birmingham, B15 2TT

To complete the instructor weekend successfully, you must attend all scheduled sessions and complete them satisfactorily.

The weekend has two components. First, there is a BLS Provider course, during which you will review your resuscitation skills and demonstrate your ability to perform them effectively. This is followed by a BLS Instructor course, where you will learn and practise how to be an effective instructor.

Upon successful completion of the instructor weekend, you will need to demonstrate your ability to be an effective instructor on at least two courses before you are officially recognised as a European Resuscitation Council Instructor.

Pre-course reading

There are two manuals you should become familiar with before the weekend. One covers the basic life support skills that you must have a strong understanding of. The other is the Instructor Course manual. You might notice some redactions and that certain pages have been removed — this is to remove outdated content scheduled for update in future editions. We will teach you the latest material during the course.

Essential resources

Please familiarise yourself with the following before the course. We recommend and expect everyone on the course to know and use these:

  • ERC 2025 Guidelines — Basic Life Support & AED (Layperson book, PDF)
  • ERC App — the official European Resuscitation Council app, for reference throughout and after the course

Small group sessions

During the instructor weekend, many sessions will be held in small groups, as you were taught during your Monday evening course. These will take place in the CM rooms. We will send you your group allocations before the course.

Meals and refreshments

Lunch is provided on both days of the weekend. There are breaks during the programme during which squash is available — please ensure you bring your own water bottle and use the water fountains.

Saturday evening social

On Saturday evening there will be a course social, featuring food, followed by a team event open to everyone at The Country Girl. CONFIRM VENUE
