First Aid Practice

The aims of this session are to describe and demonstrate first aid skills to the candidates & then facilitate their practice of these skills

Pre-session requirements
  • View first aid lecture
  • Manikins in teaching rooms (adult & paediatric / baby options)
  • Automated external defibrillators in rooms
  • Choking vests in rooms
  • Haemorrhage control equipment (eg tourniquet / bandages) in rooms
  • Clean & working equipment
  • Cleaning wipes within rooms
Running the session
Stage / RoleInstructions
Facilitate viewing of first aid lectureLecture will be delivered to the whole group and then move into small groups for catastrophic haemorrhage video and group practice Ensure screen working, your room is on the group meeting & sound works When the instructor giving lecture asks for confirmation, tell them you can see & hear (if you cannot, take appropriate steps to fix issues. The WhatsApp group will be a first port of call to flag for advice/assistance).
HandoverBegin teaching room session immediately after lecture finishes
IntroductionExplain the importance of this session as they will be teaching this to others so their skills need to be of a very high quality Explain what will happen in the session: covering the three stages of teaching: Teach it (demonstrate and talk through the skill) Facilitate it (student/candidate practice) Assess it (feedback on practice and performance relative to the expected standard).
First aid skills Choking Bleeding Recovery position Drowning PaediatricsIt is recommended to divide the group so that different skills are taught in a carousel style Demonstrate first aid skills (real time run through, including discussion and explanation)
Facilitate itDemonstrate first aid skills (start at one end of the group and ask each candidate in turn the next step in the algorithm/talk through the skill. After they have explained perform the action) Make sure they clarify key points that need it If they get it wrong, correct them & move on. If they do not know, move on to the next candidate
Facilitate it and assess itInstructor gives scenario Make sure set is put back each time but NOT whilst giving feedback. Use the learning conversation for feedback If student has done multiple things to improve on, tell them about the major 3 and leave the rest for their next turn. DO NOT give them to the student all at once
Move onto next skillRotate within the group so that all skills are covered in same manner
Conclusion/CloseBring the group back together Any questions about the session Closure: Make sure these are the last things you say to the candidates Tell candidates if they have been successful, or not, before the leave the teaching room
EndTidy room, clean manikins Instructor debrief Provide feedback about every candidate via the course feedback form
