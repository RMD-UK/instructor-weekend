Delivering A Lecture (Group Discussion Facilitator)

The aim of this session is to allow candidates to understand how teaching points can be more effectively through preparation, effective communication and consideration of students’ needs.

Pre-session requirements
  • Awareness of topics discussed by each group
  • Have copy of ‘lecture – feedback form’ from RMD pack
  • Confirm Zoom connectivity
Running the session
Stage / RoleInstructions
Co-ordinate feedback from every group - signpost throughGroups discuss one of the following each: 1 = Preparation 2 = teaching skills/communication (verbal) 3 = teaching skills/communication (non-verbal) & closure 4 = use of visual aids Ask each group’s spokesperson to summarise their discussion giving examples of the topic they critiqued (from above) Use the ‘lecture – feedback form’ as a guide. If there is something from the form that they do not cover, ask if they can think of an example from the 5-minute lecture
Summarise sessionShort summary Do not aim to repeat all points groups have raised. Much better to point out that good lecture, for example, requires much preparation, attention to Preparation, delivery of teaching & closure, and an example of this has been given in the 5-minute demonstration At end of summary, hand over to course director
