Skills Teaching (Demonstration)

The aim of this session is to allow candidates to observe the 3-step method of teaching a skill being performed.

Pre-session requirements
  • This session needs more than one person (names in programme):
  • Facilitator
  • Instructor
  • Candidate
  • 3 other members of class
  • All members of group must meet before session to practice.
  • The RMD senior acting as reviewer must also be present to confirm demonstration is correct
  • Collect manikin and mat to be used. Confirm working. Put on stage
  • Arrange chairs on stage
  • Confirm microphones work
  • Instructor must have copy of ‘skill station – feedback form’ from RMD pack
Running the session

InstructorTalk to the class – not the audience Teach chest compression technique using the 3-stage approach. Be clear in your demonstration about set – dialogue – closure. You must use the following: Position = centre of the chest Rate = 100 – 120 per minute Depth = 5 - 6cm Quantity = 30 Do not use terms like “about”, “roughly”, etc. Be succinct and precise using the terms above.
